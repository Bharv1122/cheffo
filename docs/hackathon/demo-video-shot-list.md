# Demo Video — Suggested Script & Shot List

Target length: **2–3 minutes.** Screen-record cheffodoggo.com (desktop or phone — the phone/PWA view looks great and stands out). Talk over it naturally; this script is a guide, not a teleprompter.

## Before recording

- Use a clean browser profile (no bookmarks bar clutter, no notifications).
- Have one dog profile already saved **with an allergy and a medication** (e.g., allergic to chicken, on a blood thinner) — that's what makes the safety demo land.
- Have a saved recipe with the vet-approval badge ready to show, so you don't wait on flows mid-recording.

## Shot list

| # | ~Time | On screen | What to say (gist) |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 0:00–0:15 | Landing page, scroll the hero | "This is Cheffo Doggo — it builds homemade dog food recipes personalized to your dog, and gets them approved by your own vet. Fresh-food subscriptions cost $200 a month; this is the version for people willing to cook." |
| 2 | 0:15–0:35 | Dog profile form | "Everything starts with your dog: weight, age, breed, activity, allergies — and medications. Remember that last one." |
| 3 | 0:35–1:00 | Recipe type picker → generate a full meal | "Five modes: full meals, weekly batches, toppers, treats, and Pantry Mode — build from what's already in your kitchen. Let's make a full meal." |
| 4 | 1:00–1:30 | Recipe detail: ingredients + nutrition panel | "Portions are in real kitchen units — cups and tablespoons, scaled to my dog's calories. Per-cup and per-day nutrition, checked against AAFCO targets for her life stage." |
| 5 | 1:30–1:55 | **The safety moment** — show a blocked/warned ingredient | "Here's the part I care most about: every recipe runs through a toxic-ingredient database AND a drug interaction checker. My dog's on a blood thinner, so fish oil gets hard-blocked automatically." |
| 6 | 1:55–2:20 | Vet approval section + badge / PDF | "One click sends my actual vet a one-page form — takes them about a minute. The recipe then carries a real 'Approved by Dr. ___, DVM' badge, with a PDF export." |
| 7 | 2:20–2:40 | Ask Cheffo Doggo chat — ask "how do I cook carrots?" | "The AI assistant knows this specific dog. And it gives real answers — steam 8 to 10 minutes, not 'cook them.'" |
| 8 | 2:40–3:00 | Shopping list / cooking mode, then pricing page | "Shopping list, step-by-step cooking mode. One treat recipe free, then $8 a month. It's live at cheffodoggo.com." |

## If you only have 60 seconds

Shots 1 → 3 → 5 → 6 → 8. The safety block (5) and vet badge (6) are the two things no other entry will have — never cut those.

## Bonus footage

10 seconds of your actual dog eating a Cheffo bowl at the start or end beats any feature. Judges are human.
